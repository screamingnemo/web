There are 3 online locations the playlists can be found. SoundCloud, Spotify, YouTube.

Besides playing and talking about music, I also talk about what I did and what I am currently doing. Highlighting and analyzing my past and most recent mistakes, total failures, and successes navigating life and this thing called the “modern music business” on YouTube. This initiative aims to provide some insight and practical application that ideally could guide and assist independent musicians just starting out.
